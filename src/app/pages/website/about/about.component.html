About Us

Welcome to MOTANKA, the place where tradition and the spirit of the past intertwine with modern style and comfort.

    Our story began in the toughest of times, when the world was shaken by war and people's hearts were filled with worry. It was in these moments that Maria, a brave and talented young woman, decided to create something special. She started making protective dolls, which went to the front lines alongside our defenders. These dolls carried not only hope and support, but also a piece of Ukrainian culture and traditions.

As time went by, Maria's dolls became a symbol not only of protection and luck, but also of beauty and pride. People around began to turn to them not only as talismans, but also as interior objects filled with deep meaning and history. The dolls became an integral part of the Ukrainian home, symbolizing its connection to traditions and roots.

Today, MOTANKA invites you to the world of Ukrainian beauty and warmth. Here you will find not only protective dolls, but also a variety of handmade items reflecting the richness of our culture. We take pride in the opportunity to preserve and transmit the legacy of our ancestors, making it accessible to modern generations.

Come visit us and immerse yourself in the atmosphere of genuine Ukrainian hospitality and beauty. MOTANKA is your reliable guide in the world of traditions and inspiration.
